About Connor Hearn Photography

Connor Hearn was born in March 1997 during a blizzard in lehigh county, PA. Ever since Connor was a little boy he has always loved nature, wildlife, and people all around him. During his late teens-early adulthood, Connor found his love for photography. Connor has worked for people in his personal life for years now and is ready to branch off. Connor's abilities include but are not limited to, nature photography, video recording for local musicians, and photoshoots.
